(un)veil:
The Exhibition

Open today, 8.30am to 9.30pm

Location
81 Nanyang Drive, School of Art, Design and Media
Level
1
Price
Free admission

Held annually, this year’s collaborative exhibition has been titled “(Un)veil” by the group of local artists behind it.

Made across a period of 3 months, (Un)veil was created with the study of how people interact with and view art, playing with the idea of the artists’ true intentions versus the projection of one’s personal thoughts.
